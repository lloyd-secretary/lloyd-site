Excomm Minutes 22-23

Excomm meets weekly and keeps Lloyd running smooth. The minutes are how you can keep up with how things are run in Lloyd. If you have any concerns, you can either come to the Excomm meeting (see calendar) or use the anonymous feedback form sent before each meeting.

Shed Reservation Link

This is link to book the shed (you can also find this from scanning the QR code on the door of the shed). Reserving will make it easier to schedule movies or game nights with your friends. Outside of reserved times, the shed will still be available for "walk-in" events.

Reimbursement Form

If you made a purchase that was approved to get funding, fill out this form to be reimbursed. In general, you can get reimbursed for things you purchased for events if the purchases were approved ahead of time during an excomm meeting.
