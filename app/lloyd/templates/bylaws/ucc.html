LASR/PA Selection
passed May 6, 1993
amended April 6, 1994
amended January 5, 2005
amended May 8, 2012
amended February 21, 2013
amended January 12, 2020
  1. The Lloyd Alley Support Representative Committee shall consist of one member per alley (LASR) and additional Peer Advocates (PAs), chosen separately.
  2. The LASRs shall be appointed by the Executive Committee during the third term before the end-of-year roompicks. Signups shall be made available at least one week. All candidates shall complete a written and/or oral interview, to be reviewed by Excomm.
  3. LASRs responsibilities will be outlined by Excomm before the selection process.
  4. An active PA who wishes to be a LASR shall be selected automatically.
  5. The LASRs will take office at the beginning of each school year.
  6. After the LASRs have been selected, they shall meet, before end-of-the-year roompicks, to choose their alleys. The procedure will be based on pick order.
  7. The committee of LASRs and PAs shall select from amongst themselves a Head LASR who will serve as a representative to Excomm and who shall attend Excomm Meetings. They will also have the tie breaking vote. In cases where the Head LASR is also a voting member of Excomm, they do not have an additional vote.
  8. Meetings of the LASR Committee and the RA shall take place regularly.
  9. The Lloyd representatives to the Peer Advocate selection committee shall be selected by Excomm.
