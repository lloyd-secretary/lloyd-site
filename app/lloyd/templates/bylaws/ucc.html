  1. The PA/LASR (Peer Advocate / Lloyd Alley Support Representative) Committee shall consist of Peer Advocates (PAs) and Lloyd Alley Support Representatives (LASRs).
  2. LASRs responsibilities will be outlined by Excomm before the selection process.
  3. All PAs are required to serve as LASRs for their respective alleys.
    1. If two PAs choose to room with each other, they may either choose to jointly be the LASR of the alley, or one of the two will take the role.
  4. In the event that there are not enough PAs to cover all alleys, all unfilled LASR positions will be filled by applications by non-PAs. These LASRs shall be appointed by the Executive Committee during the third term before the end-of-year roompicks. Signups shall be made available at least one week. All candidates shall complete a written and/or oral interview, to be reviewed by Excomm. Candidates must disclose if they will be absent for any term of the following academic year.
  5. The LASRs will take office at the beginning of each school year.
  6. After the LASRs have been selected, they shall meet, before end-of-the-year roompicks, to choose their alleys. The procedure will be based on pick order of the highest pick of the PA/LASR and their roommate.
  7. The committee of LASRs and PAs shall select from amongst themselves a Head PA/LASR who will serve as a representative to Excomm and who shall attend Excomm Meetings. In cases where the Head PA/LASR is also a voting member of Excomm, they do not have an additional vote.
  8. Meetings of the PA/LASR Committee and the RA shall take place regularly.
  9. In the event that a PA/LASR is absent for a term, Excomm shall select a temporary LASR from the existing alley residents to lead the alley and any alley-related events for the term.
