LASR/PA Selection
passed May 6, 1993
amended April 6, 1994
amended January 5, 2005
amended May 8, 2012
amended February 21, 2013
amended January 12, 2020
  1. The LASRs shall be appointed by the Executive Committee during the third term before the end-of-year roompicks. Signups shall be made available at least one week. All candidates shall complete a written interview, to be reviewed by Excomm.
  2. LASRs responsibilities will be outlined by Excomm before the selection process.
  3. The LASRs will take office at the beginning of each school year.
  4. After the LASRs have been selected, they shall meet, before end-of-the-year roompicks, to choose their alleys. The procedure will be based on pick order.
  5. The committee of PAs shall select from amongst themselves a representative to Excomm, who shall attend Excomm Meetings. They will also have the tie breaking vote.
  6. The Lloyd representatives to the Peer Advocate selection committee shall be selected by Excomm.
